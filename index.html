About

Tom Chan | ARB | RIBA

As a UK registered architect, I'm currently working at Foster + Partners in London, UK, I'm involved in various projects such as luxury resort village and large-scale international airport in Saudi Arabia. Before that I also worked on the construction stage of Hong Kong Palace Museum.

I hold an AA Diploma (MArch) and BArch at the University of Hong Kong. I also run a multimedia design service that specialises in animated architectural visualisation.
