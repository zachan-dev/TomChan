About

Tom holds a Master of Architecture (AA Diploma) at the AA School of Architecture, and a Bachelor of Architecture at the University of Hong Kong. He worked in Peter Cook Architects as a visualizer, for various projects.

His works were published by AArchitecture Magazine in London, NFA Architecture in Berlin and International Design Seminar at TU Delft, Netherlands.
